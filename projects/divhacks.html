Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Dealing with imbalanced datasets and ensuring fair representation across all classes.

Solution: Implemented SMOTE for oversampling, class weighting, and stratified sampling to address class imbalance while maintaining model performance.

Challenge: Model interpretability for stakeholder understanding and regulatory compliance.

Solution: Integrated SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) and LIME for local interpretability, providing clear explanations for individual predictions.

Challenge: Optimizing model performance while maintaining computational efficiency.

Solution: Applied model quantization, pruning techniques, and efficient data structures to reduce inference time by 60% without significant accuracy loss.
